Welcome to the Data Portal of the Atlas of Living Australia (ALA). For an introduction to the capabilities of the Portal, see the online tutorial.

The Atlas of Living Australia is a five-year project funded under the Australian Government's National Collaborative Research Infrastructure Strategy (NCRIS).

Its mission is to develop a biodiversity data management system which will link Australia's biological knowledge with its scientific and agricultural reference collections and other custodians of biological information.

For more information about ALA as an organisation, visit our home page at www.ala.org.au.

ALA makes available data that are shared by data providers from within Australia. These data are shared according to the ALA Data Use Agreement, which includes the provision that users of any ALA data will always give credit to the original providers.
